On April 4 at 7:30 p.m., as part of the Coteaux weekend, Overflow will host version 2 of Dimitri Geukenne's Calm
performance.

With Calm, Dimitri Geukenne offers an immersive performance where boxing becomes a circuit between the body, sound, and
light. Each blow transforms the energy of the gesture into sound and light impulses, in an experience made up of breath,
tension, and impact.

_With the support of the Department of Culture of the City of Liège._
